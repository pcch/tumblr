What is Campus House?

Campus House is a community of Jesus followers in the middle of Purdue's campus that has progressive worship, Biblical teaching, and no denominational walls.

What happens here?

We have Sunday services (8:30am, 10:00am, 11:30am, 7:00pm); a midweek Bible Study (Thursdays @ 7:30), small groups throughout the week, and all kinds of events, opportunities to serve in the community, and ways to connect.

Watch 60 Second Stories from students and alumni about their experience at Campus House.

How do I get involved?

Show up. Go to things. If you're looking for community, join a Community Group.  If you'd like to serve, get plugged in to a Mosaic. Find something you're passionate about-- something that challenges you.

Where is Campus House?

The ugly, black, mushroom-looking building across from Lilly Hall on the corner of State St. & Waldron.
