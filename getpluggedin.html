Community Groups

This is where life really happens at Campus House. A Community Group is a small group of 6-9 students who meet weekly to intentionally support, encourage, and challenge each other in their journeys with Christ. These groups provide a more personal setting within our larger Campus House body where members can share one another’s joys and struggles as they grow in their love for God and each other.

If you would like to know more or would like to join a group please contact Ken

GREYMOB

Mob: Short for “Mobilize,” as in the 3rd part of Campus House‘s mission of Reach, Prepare, & Mobilize
Grey: As in “the grey area” between the church and the culture- in order to affect both simultaneously


GreyMob is a division of Campus House which empowers college students to put their faith into action. Through events like Engaging Culture and Vision Week, and tools such as the GreyMob area of the lobby and the ongoing “windows” units, we desire to inspire students to always be thinking of how their lives, interests, and fields of study overlap with the world‘s needs.


We then come alongside passionate students who have big ideas and give them the resources and guidance to start new, faith-inspired, and socially-conscious initiatives. Our hope and prayer is that we can support and equip students who want to make a difference in the world and leverage their lives for God‘s glory.


Some of GreyMob's Projects

Liz Alig- a fashion label out of Indianapolis that recycles old clothing into high fashion through fair trade partners around the world.

Amaguru- working to design low-cost prosthetic legs that are tailored to the unique needs of those in developing countries.

Go Kids- an after-school mentoring program that invites university students to help local at-risk youth learn scientific principles and then build an electric go-cart.

Swipe Out Starvation- providing awareness of hunger issues locally and around the world, working with campus food services to give students a way to make a difference here at Purdue.
