Community Groups

This is where life really happens at Campus House. A Community Group is a small group of 6-9 students who meet weekly to intentionally support, encourage, and challenge each other in their journeys with Christ. These groups provide a more personal setting within our larger Campus House body where members can share one another’s joys and struggles as they grow in their love for God and each other.

If you would like to know more or would like to join a group please contact Ken

Each individual piece may not seem that significant on its own, but when combined with all the other pieces, it becomes essential for completing that beautiful picture. Campus House Mosaic is a group of diversely gifted individuals who serve God in various ways at Campus House and in our community. We need unique people, like yourself, to volunteer your God-given gifts to help us complete the beautiful picture of the body of Christ here at Campus House.
