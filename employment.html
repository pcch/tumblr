What is Campus House?

A campus ministry in West Lafayette serving Purdue's campus by reaching out and creating a context for deep, gospel-centered community. We embrace the arts as a common language and expression of God's love for us through worship, outreach, and studying the word of God. We have Sunday services for about 1,000 students, a mid-week bible study, small groups, and are actively involved in missions and service locally and globally. Our coffee shop is located in the heart of campus and has over 150,000 patrons every year. We come alongside students to further construct their faith in a way that is unique, filled with passion, and pointed purposefully at serving The Kingdom wherever their career takes them.

What is Greymob?

A collective of designers, developers, and strategists devoted to gathering around things that matter, things that shape our culture, and things that make a difference.

We are a non-profit design studio and business accelerator owned by Purdue Christian Campus House, a non-denominational Christian campus ministry on the campus of Purdue University.

We draw upon the enthusiasm and innovation of a university campus, coming alongside students to further construct and apply their faith in a way that is unique, practical, and pointed purposefully at serving the Kingdom wherever their career takes them. We look for clients with great ideas and great intentions, then we take them through a process of defining, researching, ideating, and mapping their passions. In short, we make things to help those who want to make things to help others.
