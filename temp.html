What is Campus House?

  • Campus House is a community of Jesus followers in the middle of Purdue's campus that has progressive worship, Biblical teaching, and no denominational walls.

What happens there?

  • We have Sunday services; a midweek Bible Study: Thursdays @ 7:30, small groups throughout the week, and all kinds of events, opportunities to serve in the community, and ways to connect.

How do I get involvoed?

  • Show up. Go to things. If you're looking for community, join a Community Group.  If you'd like to serve, get plugged into an Mosaic. Find something you're passionate about-- something that challenges you.

Where is Campus House?

  • The ugly, black, mushroom-looking building across from Lilly Hall on the corner of State St. & Waldron.
